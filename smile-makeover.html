Smile Makeover
Facial Harmony

Smile Makeover is a customized dental treatment plan designed to improve the appearance of your smile by addressing concerns related to teeth, gums, and overall facial harmony.At V Cure Dental, we combine advanced cosmetic dentistry with precision and care to create beautiful, natural-looking smiles

  • Discolored or stained teeth
  • Crooked or misaligned teeth
  • Gaps between teeth
  • Chipped, cracked, or worn teeth
  • Uneven gum line or gummy smile
TREATMENT COMPONENTS

Whats Included?

Dental Veneers

Ultra-thin ceramic shells bonded to the front of teeth to correct color, shape, and alignment issues.

Laser Whitening

In-office professional whitening that brightens your teeth by several shades in just 45 minutes.

Gum Contouring

Laser reshaping of the gum line to fix "gummy smiles" and create a more symmetrical appearance.

Laser gum depigmentation

Remove dark melanin pigment gums through laser from restore natural light pink gums

Dental Implants

An implant is a screw-like material made of titanium that replaces the root portion of a natural tooth.

Zirconia / DMLS Crowns

Zirconia crowns that are custom-milled to match the exact shade and shape of your surrounding teeth.
